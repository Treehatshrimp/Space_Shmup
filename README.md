We completed all the contents in both part one and two of the space shump demo. We decided on a few small changes to clean the game up for the coolness factor. The most major frontend change was implementing a score counter and assigning what we believe to be balanced score values to each enemy type. The score counter appears in the top left corner and updates as enemies are destroyed while resetting if the player dies. A few other changes include updating the background scroll fx to look just slightly clearer as we thought it was a bit noisy. We also added trails to the gun particles to make them look more like sci fi lasers. Then we added con trials to the hero ship, almost replicating the look you see on Star Wars tie fighters along with a trail that follows the engine to look like the burn off or energy that emits from it. These VFX for the flight tail make the player movement stand out more and help out the player in giving a sense of movement when combined with the scrolling background. The score counter lets players have a real goal to work towards as it means taking out an enemy proves value outside of the change of powerups. The sound effect we added for firing your weapons also adds a bit of a retro touch while also obviously, adding audio feedback to the players input.
